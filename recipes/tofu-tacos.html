Tofu Tacos

Image of Tofu Tacos

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Salsa
  • Avocado
  • Tortilas
  • Lettuce
  • Beans

Steps

  1. Press tofu for at least 30 minutes
  2. Pan fry tofu in water or oil adding your favorite taco seasoning(s)
  3. Heat up beans
  4. Cube avocado
  5. Chop lettucs
  6. Heat up tortillas
  7. Combine and enjoy!
