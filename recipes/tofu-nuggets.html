Tofu Nuggets

Image of tofu nuggets

Ingredients

  • Hard tofu
  • Favorite shake and bake mix
  • Onion soup mix
  • Unsweeted vegan milk
  • Flour
  • Favorite spices
  • Vinegar
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Freeze tofu
  2. Defrost tofu in fridge
  3. Press tofu for 30+ minutes
  4. Tear tofu into bite sized pieces
  5. Combine onion soup mix, spices, soy sauce, and milk
  6. Soak tofu pieces in mixture for at least one hour
  7. Combine flour and favorite soups and some soup mix in bowl
  8. Squeeze tofu to remove excess liquid
  9. Combine vinegar, milk, and spices in bowl
  10. Coat tofu in flour, then put in vinegar/milkspice combo, then coat in shake and bake
  11. Pan fry in oil or bake in oven
